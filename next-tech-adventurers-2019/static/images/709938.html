    9 Places To Visit In Hulhumale Which Are Like A Poetic Justice To The Beauty Of The Island!

    ..
    SHARES

    8 Nov 2021

    Written by

    Ankur Chowdhury

    Hulhumale Island is one of the groups of the Maldives islands; it is an artificial island created in the south of North Male Atoll, Maldives. The island of Hulhumale has beautiful gifts of nature like beaches, excellent and pleasant weather, serenity and tranquillity which attracts several tourists to the place. In addition to this, you can also try many water sports in Hulhumale such as scuba diving, snorkeling, and canoeing. While there are many things that you can enjoy, there are also many awesome places that can be explored.
    The places to visit in Hulhumale are incredibly beautiful and alluring and each of them provides perfect recreational space for you to relax and rejuvenate as you straighten your sour muscles. Here, we have a fun guide for you with the splendid places that you must visit in Hulhumale.

    9 Best Places To Visit In Hulhumale

    Hulhumale has various tourist attractions that engage and please the visitors to the fullest. Here are some of the nine best places to visit in Hulhumale.

    1. Hulhumale Beach
    2. Hulhumale Central Park
    3. Ferry Terminal Park
    4. Eastern Beach
    5. Hulhule Island
    6. HDC Building
    7. Hukuru Miskiy
    8. Hulhumale Public Beach
    9. Hulhumale Local Shops

    1. Hulhumale Beach

    Image Source

    The island of Hulhumale is famous for its beautiful and clean Hulhumale Beach. This beach has soft white sand and a picturesque view of sunrise and sunset and the cool breeze and tranquil surroundings prove to be a perfect scenario for a beautiful and romantic vacation. Also, there are options for water sports for people who love to have an adventure on their trips and the charges of these sports are reasonable and affordable. There are several beach restaurants, and cafes with good quality food and beverages served. Overall, this beach is a great place to enjoy a holiday with family and friends.

    Location: Beach Road, Hulhumale, Mal, Maldives
    Timings: Open 24 hours
    Entry Fees: N/A

    Must Read: 9 Best Things To Do In Hulhumale For A One-Of-Its-Kind 2022 Holiday Experience

    2. Hulhumale Central Park

    Image Source

    The Hulhumale Central Park is a park located in the central region of Hulhumale Island. It has ample greenery and a clean environment which serves to be a significant factor for relaxation in Hulhumale. Though the place is small, there are a few local sights inside it which are frequently visited by the tourists – there is a beautiful fountain with a jar structure and also a fishpond with a small bridge. People can walk around in the park, play some outdoor games or simply relax on the grass there. This Park also hosts some events like the Marathon and other such sports events.

    Location: Mal, Maldives
    Timings: Open 24 hours
    Entry Fees:

    • For Adults: INR 10
    • For Children: INR 5

    Suggested Read: Airport Beach Hotel Hulhumale

    3. Ferry Terminal Park

    Ferry Terminal Park is one of the best places in Hulhumale to just sit back, relax and enjoy the day. One can find exciting amusement activities in this park. There are two kiosk eateries in the Ferry Terminal Park where you can find typical Maldivian snacks that are fresh and delicious. Overall, this park is a great place to enjoy, especially for a family with kids.

    Location: Male, Maldives
    Timings: 8 am to 11 pm
    Entry Fees: N/A

    Suggested Read: 27 Best Beaches In Maldives For A Relaxing Time With Your Partner In 2022

    4. Eastern Beach

    Image Source

    The Eastern Beach of Hulhumale is a beautiful site that attracts several tourists around the year. The soft white sand and the cool breeze flowing all over have nothing but absolute peace to offer. Also, there are varieties of water sports available here such as scuba diving or snorkeling, which lets you explore the beautiful and colorful marine life to the fullest. This beach is indeed a great place to enjoy and relax.

    Location: Hulhumale, Maldives
    Timings: Open all 24 hours
    Entry Fees: N/A

    Suggested Read: 50 Pristine Places To Visit In Maldives For An Amazing Vacay In 2022!

    5. Hulhule Island

    Image Source

    This island is very close to the capital island of Male and there is a bridge connecting these two islands. This bridge has two lanes- one is for motorcycles, whereas the other one is for pedestrians. The most advantageous part of this island is that there is an international airport situated on Hulhule Island and this Velena International Airport connects the Maldives to the rest of the world via airways. People also take ferries to islands nearby from Hulhule Island.

    Location: Maldives
    Timings: Open all 24 hours
    Entry Fees: N/A

    Suggested Read: 6 Incredible Indian Restaurants In Maldives To Visit On Your Next Maldives Trip!

    6. HDC Building

    Image Source

    The HDC stands for Housing Development Corporation, and its building is called the HDC Building. It was established in 2001 and is the center for all kinds of development decisions related to industries, commercial and residential infrastructure, hospitality and recreation. The land of Hulhumale is governed through this office and the grandeur and finesse of this structure are incredible! The tourists visit this place to view the government office here and understand the working.

    Location: Huvandhumaa Hingun, Mal, Maldives
    Timings: 9 am to 5 pm
    Entry Fees: N/A

    Suggested Read: Artificial Beach Maldives: All You Need To Know Before You Go

    7. Hukuru Miskiy

    Also known as the Old Friday Mosque, the structure of the Hukuru Miskiy mosque is elaborate and excellent. The intricate designs made on the domes and the ornate architecture of the mosque is worth applause. This mosque was built in 1656 and is one of the oldest structures in the Maldives and Hulhumale. This coral stone building is an excellent example of sea-culture architecture and all those who love to marvel at the history and architectural wonders will find this place really interesting. It is frequently visited by tourists, especially with families from all over the world.

    Location: Male, Maldives
    Timings: 9 am to 5 pm
    Entry Fees: N/A

    Suggested Read: Maldives Vs Seychelles For Honeymoon: Where Would You Like To Go In 2022?

    8. Hulhumale Public Beach

    Image Source

    This beach is an excellent place for all those who love to enjoy themselves on the soft sand and cool waters. This beach has tranquillity like none other. Even though it is a public beach, cleanliness and hygiene quotient is maintained. You can also find options for adventure water sports like diving, snorkeling and scuba diving at the Hulhumale Public Beach. This place surely serves to be the best choice for all those who love serenity blended with adventure.

    Location: Beach Rd, Mal, Maldives
    Timings: Open all 24 hours
    Entry Fees: N/A

    Suggested Read: Maldives Or Bora Bora For Honeymoon: Which One Should You Go To In 2022?

    9. Hulhumale Local Shops

    The island of Hulhumale is very small and has a limited population. That is the reason why there are no huge malls and shopping complexes over here. But this place does have options for all those who love shopping. There are a variety of local shops offering the best of local souvenirs. One can buy things like cane baskets, showpieces made from seashells, traditional mats, ceramic plates, and many such products. Also, one can find eatables like dried fish chips, various types of marinated fish and preserved pickles. All of these prove to be great things that one can take back from Hulhumale Island.

    Location: Hulhumale, Maldives
    Timings: 9 am to 10 pm
    Entry Fees: N/A

    Further Read: Maldives In June A Guide To Holidaying At The Tropical Island

    There are many scenic and beautiful places to visit in Hulhumale Island where you can enjoy yourself with your friends and family as you get to know more about the culture of Maldives. You can take many tours to fully explore all these places and may even want to extend your trip as exploring these places will keep you entertained for days! So, plan your trip to the Maldives soon and enjoy what Hulhumale has to offer.
