Fish Market

Posted on: 2/19/2021, 7:05:46 AM
Maldivian Fishermen around Male atoll bring their daily catch to the Main fish market. This is sold in attractive prices to the locals and Suppliers, different sizes of Tuna, Wahoo, Sail fish and reef fish is available daily.
